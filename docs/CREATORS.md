## Selection criteria

**Include**

- Active LinkedIn presence with **substantive posts** (not only reshares).
- Archetype mix: **founder**, **operator/executive**, **practitioner** (Excel/data/automation), **PM/product**, **academic/research**, **media/curator**.
- Topic overlap with at least one of: **LLM-assisted work** (Claude or general gen-AI), **Excel / spreadsheets / analytics**, **B2B SaaS or enterprise adoption**, **product or GTM in tech**.

**Exclude**

- Pure consumer/lifestyle influencers with no B2B or tooling angle.
- Accounts whose primary focus is **not** English-language professional/tech content (unless Module A explicitly expands languages).
- **Company pages only** (this list is **people** for voice diversity).
- Heavily promotional/spam patterns without educational workflow content (subjective; spot-check posts).
